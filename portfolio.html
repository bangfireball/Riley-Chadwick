Get Outta My Garbage 4 Train Jam 2016

Get Outta My Garbage 4 was the game my team and I made in Train Jam 2016. What is Train Jam? Train Jam is a game jam that is on a train heading from Chicago IL, to Emeryville CA. The jam lasted 52 hours and during that time, we created Get Outta My Garbage 4. The theme was maximum capacity and throwing garbage at each other is how we took that theme. To win, all you have to do is reach the center of the stage before your opponent but each of you can only carry so much garbage. So will you spend time moving or sabotaging your opponent?

Choo Choo Spell (work in progress)

Choo Choo Spell is currently a work in progress. It is an independent project that I, and three others, were selected to work on. The game is an educational game where the player controls the train to collect letters to spell words to fuel the train and collect points. The current release date is 2016.

RitrollGlobal Game Jam 2016

For Global Game Jam 2016, the theme was 'Ritual'. Armed with this theme, i wanted to create a VR game with Google Cardboard. Ritroll was born when my friends and I just wanted to have fun with the game jam. So prepare for a dark atmosphere and potential spoilers.

A Journey to YouGlobal Game Jam 2015

The theme for the 2015 Global Game Jam was 'What do I do now?'. My partner and I thought about the theme for a while before we settled on the idea that would become A Journey to You. Two lovers are separated and throughout the game they are trying to find each other. The game is a 2D mirror platformer.
